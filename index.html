ABOUT US

 ** NSN Group Of School's **

We are one of the best school's in South Chennai 'The NSN Group Of Schools'. We have 2 school's. One in Chromepet and another in Chitlapakam. One school has two boards state board and CBSE. NSN recently celebrated it's Golden Jubilee. The founder of NSN is Mrs Lalitha menon and the Correspondent is Mrs. Chithra Prasad.

What's NEW !


  • Arranging special Colour Day for Kinder garden children.

    On colour day children are supposed to wear dresses in the given colour like pink, blue and many more. Children have to bring toys or things in that colour and speak a few words about it . There will be no classes to attend on that day for them . They will enjoy and spend a good time .

  • Engaging students to participate in various competions .

    Our school conducts many competitions. Our teacher's convince us to join theese competions. So, we can improve our skills. The 3 children who do their best are the winners.
